Climate Finance Gap Analysis

GAP

$170B Annual Shortfall

India needs $223B/year for NDC commitments; current flows are ~$49B annually. 76% gap remains unfunded.

CHALLENGE

Adaptation Underfunded

Only 6% of climate finance goes to adaptation. Mitigation receives 94% despite equal urgency.

CHALLENGE

Domestic Capital Constraints

Banks face asset-liability mismatch for 20-year climate projects. Green bond market nascent at $20B.

OPPORTUNITY

Blended Finance Models

First-loss capital from philanthropy can de-risk commercial investment. 1:4 to 1:8 leverage ratios possible.
